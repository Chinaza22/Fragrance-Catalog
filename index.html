A Hayati Perfume

A Hayati by Al Haramain Perfumes is an Amber Floral fragrance for women and men. The nose behind this fragrance is Christian Carbonnel. Top notes are Musk and Amber; the middle notes are Musk, Rose, and Sugar; base notes are Musk, Woody Notes and Agarwood (Oud). Product Name. HAYATI.

Ajwad

#9000

Perfume Ajwad Eau de Parfum by Lattafa Perfumes is a sweet, fruity-floral fragrance for women. Fruity and light, the top note opens with lychee, bergamot and fruity notes. Jasmine, roses and cinnamon fill the floral and warm heart notes.

Amber&Leather

#10000

Amber & Leather Evoking the heat of the desert landscape of the west, the fragrance reveals itself like layers of sand blown in the wind. The combination of leather with warm amber, floral and spicy notes, produce an irresistible and dark fragrance. The matt black cylinder is cool and edgy in its appearance. It is the perfect perfume for men in its scent and appearance.
